European Salad

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces chicken breast (previously sliced)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt


  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 3 green onions (sliced)
  • 1 large yellow bell pepper, sliced (about 1 1/2 cups)
  • 1 - 10-ounce mixed European lettuce


  • 1-1/2 cups whole wheat croutons (about 24 croutons)

  • Fresh ground pepper to taste

Steps

Step 1


Place all ingredients for the vinaigrette in a blender, and blend until smooth; set vinaigrette aside. (Note: Vinaigrette can be made ahead of time and refrigerated.)

Step 2


Season the chicken with salt and pepper.

Step 3


Heat oil in a large skillet.

Step 4


Sauté chicken until cooked through; remove from pan and let cool.

Step 5


Cut the onions into slices.

Step 6


Toss cut vegetables with European green salad mix.

Step 7


On individual plates, top salad with chicken and croutons, drizzle with dressing, and finish with fresh cracked pepper, if desired.

Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 Boiled eggs
  • 2 large tomatoes


  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 Red onion
  • 120 Grams of feta cheese
  • 15 Kalamata Olives
  • 1 Teaspoon dried oregano


  • Parsley, arugula or fresh watercress to taste

  • 1 Tablespoon extra virgin olive oil

  • 1 Tablespoon of white vinegar

  • Salt to taste.

Steps

Step 1

Cook the eggs for 6 to 8 minutes. Remove and cool in cold water.

Step 2

Cut the tomatoes into wedges, the cucumber into slices and the onion into rings.

Step 3

Mix all these ingredients in a bowl.

Step 4

Drain the feta cheese and cut it into cubes, add to the bowl along with the olives and dried oregano.

Step 5

Dress with a vinaigrette of extra virgin olive oil and vinegar to taste, with a little salt.

Step 6

Serve garnished with sliced hard-boiled eggs and sprinkled with parsley, arugula or chopped watercress.

Israeli Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 Medium cucumbers
  • 4 Large tomatoes


  • 1 Green or red bell pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Za'atar – optional
  • Salt and pepper to taste.


Steps

Step 1

Leave the vegetables out of the refrigerator until they reach room temperature.

Step 2

Chop all vegetables and put in a large bowl.

Step 3

Just before serving, toss with the olive oil, lemon juice, and za'atar (a spice mix that includes the herb along with toasted sesame seeds, dried sumac, and salt that you can find at grocery stores). East).
