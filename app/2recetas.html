Chickenüçó with beerüç∫ and mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken cut into pieces or 4 thighs with thigh.
  • 1 Onion
  • 8 Mushrooms.
  • 33 CL of beer.üç∫
  • Homemade chicken broth to cover the chicken or, if you don't have it, water. Usually less than a liter is needed. You can prepare homemade chicken broth with our recipe.
  • 1 Tablespoon of fine corn flour or cornstarch.
  • Olive oil, salt and freshly ground black pepper.


Steps

Step 1


Season the chicken pieces on both sides.

Step 2

We are going to prepare the recipe with our pressure cooker but if you do not have one, follow all the steps exactly the same, only that at the final moment of cooking we will tell you how to make it with a normal pot.

Step 3

Put the pot over medium-high heat and add a splash of oil. When it is hot, add the chicken (and if it is too much for your pot, do it in two batches). Let it brown perfectly on one side, turn it over and do the same on the other. In a matter of 5-7 minutes it is usually ready, take it out of the pot and reserve it.

Step 4

Peel the onion, cut it in half and each half into thin strips.

Step 5

In the pot with the oil that the chicken has left, add the onion along with a little salt and pepper and let it cook over medium-low heat for about 5 minutes without browning or burning.

Step 6

Cut off and discard the bottom half of the mushroom stems, wash and pat dry as well. Cut them into thin slices.

Step 7

When the onion is tender, add the mushrooms and cook everything together for 5 more minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 8

Return the chicken to the pot and pour in the beer. Increase the heat so that it is high and the alcohol evaporates, it will take about 5 minutes or until the smell of alcohol stops.

Step 9

Now add the broth or enough water to almost completely cover the ingredients (you will get quite a bit of sauce).

Step 10

Now it's time to cook the recipe. You have two options, slow and fast (and for the record, both are equally delicious, there is no difference in terms of flavor and final result): If you use a regular pot, keep the heat on high until the broth starts to boil, at which point reduce the heat to a minimum and let it cook for about 30 minutes or until the chicken is tender. If you use a pressure cooker like we do, close it and put the heat on high. When you have the valve raised or you can see the two pressure rings, reduce the heat and let it cook for 5 minutes (it will depend on the brand of pot, ours with 5 is enough). The temperature of the fire must allow the pressure not to drop but not to rise during that time. When the time is up, remove the pot from the heat and let the steam come out little by little until it runs out of pressure with the spout or valve completely lowered and you can open it.

Step 11

When your chicken with beer is ready, taste it to correct it with salt and pepper. And if the sauce has run out and you want to thicken it (we always thicken it a little), mix a tablespoon of cornstarch in 4 tablespoons of water and add it to the pot. Give it a good stir and let it thicken in the remaining heat.

Baked Sardines with garlic and parsley

Ingredientes

  • 1 kg of sardines
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Afew leaves of parsley
  • 1 lemon
  • Fine salt and coarse salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

Step 1


The most important thing is to choose the fish. Nothing to choose some sardines already filleted or frozen. They must be very fresh, whole, with a bright color, with bright eyes and "glue the eye".

Step 2

With them already at home, we don't have to clean them. This may be strange but, made with the intestines, it is the juiciest they are. Without them, sardines tend to dry out in the oven. These are also very easy to remove.

Step 3

With all this clarified, we preheat the oven to 220¬∫C. While it is heating, in a mortar, put the garlic, parsley, a pinch of fine salt and a dash of good olive oil. We crush all the ingredients until we obtain a mashed with a lot of flavor.

Step 4

On a baking tray, put a piece of parchment paper, also known as baking paper. On it, we put a thin layer of coarse salt and on it, the sardines. This trick will reduce the strong smell that usually remains when grilling this fish, although it will not eliminate it completely. This done, on the sardines, we can put the mashed garlic and parsley. Another option is to reserve it for the end and put it raw. I leave this to your choice. In any case, we put the sardines in the oven, already heated to 220¬∫C and roast them for about 15 minutes. Until they are cooked. Do not overcook or they will be very dry

Step 5

After the time, we remove the sardines from the oven and serve them on a plate. If you decided not to include the mash in the oven, now would be the time to add it. It will give a stronger touch to the preparation. Accompany with 1/4 of a lemon, so that the diner can add it to their liking
